Title: Road 99

----

Text: 

The story of Road 99 tangles the biographies of many Afghans over the past decades. What is important is to draw a realistic image of young Afghans today who are in solidarity with one another, men and women who are united in their dream of a normal life with freedom of choice.

**Production: **
France, Germany, Iran 2022

**Premiere:**
Interfilm Competition November 2022
